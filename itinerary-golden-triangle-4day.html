4 Day Golden Triangle to Agra and Jaipur from Delhi

Overview

Come and fall in love with our Golden Triangle Tour 4 days, wherein we take you on a journey to Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur's ancient and famous monuments such as India Gate, Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, Taj Mahal at sunrise, Agra Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and more. Experience the magic of the Taj Mahal, the mascot of India, and Jaipur's most impressive attractions. Cherish beautiful memories as we drop you at your desired location in Delhi or Jaipur.

Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi Half Day Tour → Agra

  • Pickup from Delhi, Gurgaon, or Noida
  • Highlights:
    • Qutub Minar – 73m high, glorious Indian history
    • India Gate – All India War Memorial
    • Lotus Temple – 27 marble petals, flowerlike shape
    • Drive past Parliament & President's House
  • Evening: Expressway drive to Agra, hotel check-in
Overnight: Agra

Day 2: Agra → Jaipur

  • Sunrise: Guided tour of Taj Mahal
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Agra Fort – Red sandstone fortress
  • Drive to Jaipur, hotel check-in
Overnight: Jaipur

Day 3: Jaipur City Tour

  • Highlights:
    • Amer Fort – Red sandstone & marble
    • Jal Mahal – Palace in Man Sagar Lake
    • City Palace – 18th-century royal complex
    • Jantar Mantar – Largest sundial
    • Hawa Mahal – Palace of Winds
Overnight: Jaipur

Day 4: Jaipur → Delhi

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Check out and drive to Delhi
  • Drop-off at your desired location in Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, or Jaipur
