Golden Triangle with Ranthambore Tour Package

Overview

Experience the perfect blend of cultural heritage and wildlife adventure with our Golden Triangle with Ranthambore tour. This unique package combines the iconic Golden Triangle circuit (Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur) with an exciting wildlife safari in Ranthambore National Park, home to the majestic Royal Bengal Tigers. Witness the architectural marvels of Mughal and Rajput eras, explore ancient forts and palaces, and embark on thrilling jungle safaris in one of India's most famous tiger reserves.

Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi → Agra

  • Morning pickup from Delhi
  • Drive to Agra via Yamuna Expressway
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit Agra Fort
    • Local market exploration
  • Evening: Free time
Overnight: Agra

Day 2: Agra → Ranthambore

  • Sunrise visit to Taj Mahal
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Drive to Ranthambore
  • Evening:
    • Check-in at wildlife resort
    • Orientation about safari
Overnight: Ranthambore

Day 3: Ranthambore Safari

  • Morning:
    • Early morning jungle safari
    • Breakfast at resort
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit Ranthambore Fort
    • Evening jungle safari
Overnight: Ranthambore

Day 4: Ranthambore → Jaipur

  • Morning jungle safari
  • Breakfast and check-out
  • Drive to Jaipur
  • Evening:
    • Visit Hawa Mahal
    • Local market exploration
Overnight: Jaipur
