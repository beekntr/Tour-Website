Colourful Rajasthan with Taj Mahal Tour Package

Overview

Experience the vibrant colors and rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan combined with the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal. This comprehensive tour takes you through the most colorful cities of Rajasthan - Jaipur (Pink City), Jodhpur (Blue City), and Udaipur (White City), along with a visit to the magnificent Taj Mahal in Agra. Immerse yourself in the royal splendor of Rajasthan's palaces, forts, and vibrant markets, while also witnessing the architectural marvel of the Taj Mahal, a symbol of eternal love.

Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi → Jaipur

  • Morning pickup from Delhi
  • Drive to Jaipur
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit Hawa Mahal
    • City Palace
  • Evening: Local market exploration
Overnight: Jaipur

Day 2: Jaipur City Tour

  • Morning:
    • Amer Fort with Elephant ride
    • Jantar Mantar
  • Afternoon:
    • Jal Mahal
    • Albert Hall Museum
Overnight: Jaipur

Day 3: Jaipur → Jodhpur

  • Morning drive to Jodhpur
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit Mehrangarh Fort
    • Jaswant Thada
  • Evening: Clock Tower market
Overnight: Jodhpur

Day 4: Jodhpur → Udaipur

  • Morning drive to Udaipur
  • Afternoon:
    • City Palace
    • Jagdish Temple
  • Evening: Lake Pichola boat ride
Overnight: Udaipur

Day 5: Udaipur → Agra

  • Morning flight to Agra
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit Agra Fort
    • Local market exploration
  • Evening: Free time
Overnight: Agra

Day 6: Agra → Delhi

  • Sunrise visit to Taj Mahal
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Drive to Delhi
  • Transfer to airport/station
